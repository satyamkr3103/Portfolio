Time Moves When You Move

When You Move is a minimalist 2D puzzle game built in Unity (2022 LTS) that explores the relationship between movement and time.

The core mechanic is simple but powerful: time only moves when the player moves. When the player stops, the entire world freezes — including enemies, physics objects, and hazards.

This design forces players to plan every action carefully, turning even simple puzzles into thoughtful challenges.

Level Design:

  • Level 1 introduces the core mechanic through environmental puzzles
  • Level 2 expands spatial reasoning with longer, indirect solutions
  • Level 3 introduces enemies, adding light pressure to puzzle-solving

All puzzles are designed to be fair and readable, relying on visual clarity and player discovery rather than tutorials. The game focuses on clean systems and low-performance overhead, making it suitable for low-spec hardware.

Tech Used: Unity (2022 LTS), C#
